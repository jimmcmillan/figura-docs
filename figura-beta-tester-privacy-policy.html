Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10th July 2026

John McMillan, ABN 78 107 857 ("we", "us", "our") operates the Figura application ("the App"), including its beta testing program distributed via Apple's TestFlight.

This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

a) Crash reports and analytics

When you use the App, we may automatically collect technical information to help us diagnose problems and improve performance, including:

  • Crash logs and error reports
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and usage patterns (e.g. features used, session length)

This information does not include the audio, presets, or musical content you create with the App.

b) Beta tester information

If you participate in our TestFlight beta program, we collect:

  • Your email address (via Apple's TestFlight, used to invite you and manage your access to beta builds)
  • Any feedback, comments, bug reports, or suggestions you send us directly (e.g. via email, TestFlight feedback, or community channels, i.e., such as Discord, etc)

c) Information we do not collect

We do not collect payment information (this is handled entirely by Apple for App Store purchases), and we do not access, transmit, or store your audio projects, presets, or creative content outside your own device unless you explicitly choose to share it with us (e.g. attaching a preset file to a bug report).

2. How We Use This Information

We use the information above to:

  • Diagnose and fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues
  • Understand how the App is used in order to improve it
  • Communicate with beta testers about new builds, testing instructions, and program updates
  • Respond to feedback, support requests, and bug reports
  • Improve future versions of the App based on tester input

3. Third Parties

We use the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Apple (TestFlight, App Store Connect, crash reporting frameworks): Apple processes tester email addresses and delivers crash/analytics data on our behalf as part of the TestFlight and App Store platform. See Apple's privacy policy at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

We do not sell your information to any third party, and we do not use your information for advertising purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain beta testers' email addresses and feedback for as long as the beta program is active and for a reasonable period afterwards to support ongoing development. Crash and analytics data is retained only as long as needed to diagnose and resolve issues, per the retention practices of the analytics service used.

5. Your Rights

You may:

  • Ask us what information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or delete your information (e.g. remove your email from our beta tester list)
  • Withdraw from the beta program at any time by leaving TestFlight

To exercise any of these, contact us at figura.beta@proton.me

6. Children's Privacy

Figura is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction).

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information we hold, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the updated "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, communicated directly to beta testers.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy or your information can be sent to:
figura.beta@proton.me
